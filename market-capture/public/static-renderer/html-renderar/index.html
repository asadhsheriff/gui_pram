What is a Capsulle?

Capsulle is an intelligent promotional offer which you can save on your mobile phone with a tap, share it with your friends with a swipe.

Why Capsulle? Why not Coupons?

They show up on your mobile phone when you needed it the most. One good example is they show up at your breakfast, lunch and dinner times to let you know about a deal available in your favorite nearby restaurant.
With a single tap, you can find your friends using the Capsulle and also connect to a community sharing Capsulles. You don't have to take the pain of deleting the expired Capsulle, it just won't show up after expiration.
